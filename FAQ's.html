Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a full range of interior design services, including space planning, furniture selection, color consultation, custom cabinetry design, lighting design, and project management. We cater to both residential and commercial projects.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. We offer customized packages and hourly rates. We recommend scheduling a consultation to discuss your specific needs and receive a detailed quote.

What is your design process?

Our process typically includes an initial consultation, concept development, design presentation, revisions, and project implementation. We work closely with our clients at every stage to ensure their vision is realized.

How long does a typical project take?

The timeline varies based on the size and complexity of the project. A small residential project might take a few weeks, while a large commercial project could take several months. We provide a detailed project timeline during the initial consultation.

Do you provide 3D renderings?

Yes, we provide 3D renderings to help you visualize the final design. This allows you to make informed decisions and ensure the design meets your expectations.

How do I schedule a consultation?

Yes, we believe that plants and greenery add life and vibrancy to any space. We can incorporate indoor plants and vertical gardens to enhance your interior.

How do you handle billing and payments?

We provide detailed invoices and offer flexible payment options. We discuss payment schedules during the initial consultation.

What is your approach to client communication?

We prioritize clear and consistent communication. We provide regular updates and are always available to answer your questions.

Do you handle procurement of furniture and materials?

Yes, we handle the entire procurement process, including sourcing, purchasing, and delivery of furniture, fixtures, and materials. We work with trusted suppliers to ensure quality and timely delivery.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out the contact form on our website or by calling us directly. We will get back to you to arrange a convenient time.
